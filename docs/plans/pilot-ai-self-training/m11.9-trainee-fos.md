# M11.9 — Trainee first officers (two-pilot dynamics)

**Status:** Stub — not yet planned. Lowest priority of the M11 items.

## Why

In airline ops, the pilot monitoring (PM) handles radio while the pilot flying (PF) flies. When the PM is a low-time FO, you hear the difference: slower readbacks, occasional brain-fade, the captain sometimes interjecting a correction ("approach, sorry, AAL235 descending two thousand five hundred"). This is the most subtle of the realism items and the lowest-value individually — but it's also the cheapest to layer on once M11.1 and M11.8 exist.

## Sketch

- Subset of airline operator class only (GA is single-pilot). Per-spawn flag `IsTraineeFO`, low rate (~10% of airline spawns).
- Effects:
  - **Slower readbacks** — already covered by `PilotProficiency.Student` in M11.1; this is the airline-class analogue.
  - **Captain correction** — rare event (~1 per session per trainee crew): after a slow / incomplete readback, a second voice (slightly different TTS profile?) issues the correction. Terminal SAY line tags the second utterance distinctly.
- Purely flavor / texture. No new commands, no new scoring axes.

## Open questions

- Does this carry enough training value to justify even a small implementation? Or does M11.1's `PilotProficiency` already cover it adequately for airline aircraft?
- "Second voice" requires two TTS voices per crew — current TTS uses one speaker per aircraft (random pick from the 904-speaker pool). Adding a per-aircraft secondary voice doubles per-aircraft voice state. Possibly skip the second voice and just have the same voice correct itself, signaled by the SAY line wording ("correction —").
- How does this interact with `PilotProficiency`? Probably: `IsTraineeFO` implies `Student` proficiency for the readback path, plus the captain-correction layer.

## Out of scope (probably)

- Modelling the PF separately (the PF doesn't speak; we only ever hear the PM).
- Captain-corrects-on-an-error-the-controller-didn't-catch (overlaps with M11.3 readback errors — too entangled to be worth chasing).
- "Captain takes over the radio after the trainee struggles" — too speculative for the marginal training value.

## Pick-up notes

**Consider deferring indefinitely.** The training value is genuinely small and almost entirely covered by M11.1 (proficiency) + M11.8 (operator class) once both ship. Re-evaluate after those two land and see if there's still a real gap to fill. If we do build it, do the no-second-voice variant — captain correction signaled by SAY-line wording only, not audio.
